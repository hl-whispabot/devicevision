Quick Answers

Common questions about Device Vision and our services

What is Device Vision?

Device Vision is a subsidiary of OCK Group Berhad that specializes in transforming telecommunications data into actionable marketing intelligence for enterprises.

How can telco data help my business?

Telco data provides insights into user behavior, preferences, location patterns, and usage trends, enabling targeted marketing, contextual advertising, and data-driven strategy development.

Is the data compliant with privacy regulations?

Yes, all data is anonymized and processed in full compliance with Malaysian Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) and international privacy standards.

What industries do you serve?

We serve retail, e-commerce, finance, real estate, automotive, FMCG, and any enterprise looking to leverage data-driven marketing strategies.

How quickly can we get started?

After initial consultation and setup, most clients can begin accessing insights within 2-4 weeks, depending on data requirements.
